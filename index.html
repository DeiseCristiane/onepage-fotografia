Sobre Mim

"My passion as a guitar maker comes from a life-long obsession with making things. From a young age I have always tried to manipulate objects and materials in order to create something entirely different. I find that working with wood is a way for me to connect with nature. The simple act of shaping wood to make something functional or beautiful brings me endless satisfaction. After working as a product designer for numerous retailers over the years, and after I had my first child, I decided to take the plunge and start my own home staging business…”
